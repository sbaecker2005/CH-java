Leads CRUD

POST /api/leads
→ cadastrar lead (201 Created)
GET /api/leads/{id}
→ buscar por ID (200 / 404)
PATCH /api/leads/{id}/status
→ atualizar status com validação
DELETE /api/leads/{id}
→ remover lead (204 No Content)
